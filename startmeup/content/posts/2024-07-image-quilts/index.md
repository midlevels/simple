<p class="subtitle">Using Adam Schwartz’ Chrome browser extension</p>

The plugin permits adding images while browsing, shuffling the resulting quilt elements, grayscaling or inverting colors, and increasing or decreasing the elements to fit more or fewer into the quilt. It exports as a PNG file and automatically downloads to your computer's downloads folder once you've finished tweaking things.
