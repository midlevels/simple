A sketch of Himeji Castle on ipad Pro. Akira Kurosawa sets his movie _[Ran](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0089881/)_ in and around this, Japan's oldest castle, which dates back to 1333. The skies were overcast when we visited. But I chose to have a blue sky in this little rendering.  The people at the base of this sketch of Himeji Castle on ipad Pro are there to provide scale, but I don't know… they may be too large. Here's the photo I took and was working from:
