The Flying Pigeon™ bicycle: My first mode of transport in the PRC many years ago. The streets were quiet but for the sound of moving bike tires and bells to signal someone from behind that you were there. The fee to park your bike under the watchful eyes of a bike guard was ten fen (about 1 US cent). There were locks on them, but they were self locks, not chains, so they could have been thrown into the back of a truck … except for the bike lot and guard, who prevented such consequences.  But parking your ride inside the university walls was always safe.
