Zadie Smith authored _Intimations_, which was published in 2020 amidst the global COVID-19 pandemic. The book reflects on the unprecedented times and offers Smith's observations and reflections on various aspects of life during this period.

The essays in "Intimations" touch on a range of topics, including the impact of the pandemic, racial injustice, social inequality, and the role of art in times of crisis. Smith explores the ways in which the pandemic has exposed and exacerbated existing societal issues, prompting deeper reflections on our shared humanity and the interconnectedness of our lives.

Through her insightful and lyrical prose, Smith offers personal anecdotes, philosophical musings, and cultural analysis. She contemplates the fragility of life, the importance of community, and the power of empathy and compassion. Smith also delves into questions of privilege, social responsibility, and the ways in which the pandemic has disproportionately affected marginalized communities

_Intimations_ serves as a reflection on the state of the world and a call for introspection and collective action. Smith's essays blend personal experiences with broader societal concerns, offering readers a thought-provoking exploration of contemporary issues during a time of crisis.
