## Daily listen:

I used to buy Steve's tapes by mail order from him personally. I can't even recall how I got his address. I may have bought one tape and it was in the liner notes. He'd often include a nice note of thanks and some odd ephemera back with the ordered tapes that I enjoyed reading. At some point in the early '90s he emailed me a link to his entire digital music collection to that point to download for free, which I also appreciated. _Safe Journey_ was my first or second album of his. The other being _Yr_ (wire? year?).

Genre: **Jazz**

Year: **1983**

Favorite track: **Climbing**
