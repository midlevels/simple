Nara, the land of the beautiful Todai-ji temple, mochi makers, attack deer, and successful plots to assassinate a prime minister with a MacGyvered gun. But wait. There's more. On this day there was this nice artist, who took a break to chat with us and give us some breadcrumbs from his stockpile, which he indicated to us we should toss into the pond. The scrum of koi ensued.
