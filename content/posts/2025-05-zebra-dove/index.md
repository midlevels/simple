Another lost, now found photo from a SanDisk memory card. The zebra dove (斑姬地鸠) is found on the Thai-Malay peninsula to Java and Sumatra in Indonesia. The pale blue-grey forehead indicates this is a male. Shot at the Hong Kong Eduard Youde Aviary.
