Antarctica is Claire Keegan's first collection of short stories. 

I separate these very well-written stories into the following categories: dark, sad or scary. 
Many stories take place during the winter months or have some aspect of cold about them. Most stories deal with marginalized families or persons, mostly poor and rural. 

I read all of the stories twice.
